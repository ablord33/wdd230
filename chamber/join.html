Membership level benefits

  • NP Membership: For non profit organizations. No fee. Access to basic resources and events.
  • Bronze Membership: For small businesses. Low fee. Access to basic and premium resources and events. One spotlight position on the home page per year.
  • Silver Membership: For medium businesses. Moderate fee. Access to basic, premium, and exclusive resources and events. Two spotlight positions on the home page per year. 10% discount on event fees.
  • Gold Membership: For large businesses. High fee. Access to all resources and events. Four spotlight positions on the home page per year. 20% discount on event fees. Free training sessions.
